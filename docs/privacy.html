Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 30/07/25

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data in relation to the message summarization bot ("the Bot") available via our open-source GitHub repository.

1. Who We Are

The Bot is maintained as project by Dodo-alone. You may contact via GitHub if you have any questions.

2. What Data We Process

The Bot accesses and processes group chat messages for the sole purpose of generating summaries.

  • We do not store or retain message contents.
  • Only display names (not user IDs or permanent identifiers) are used and only during runtime.
  • No personal identifiers are logged or stored.
  • Error and configuration logs may be collected to improve functionality, but do not contain personal data.

3. Legal Basis

Under the GDPR, our legal basis for processing is legitimate interest where the privacy impact is minimal and users are informed via notice.

Where applicable, group administrators who install or authorize the Bot agree to inform users that their messages may be processed.

4. User Controls

The Bot may include an !optout command allowing individual users to exclude their messages from summarization. Where enabled, we store minimal hashed identifiers solely to honor this request.

5. Data Sharing

No personal data is shared with third parties. Messages are sent to the Google Gemini API for summarization only with anonymous user display names, and processed per Google's own privacy and safety policies.
